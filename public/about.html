About Renovo Surface Solutions

Built in Salt Lake City on a simple idea: commercial properties deserve a cleaning partner that shows up, does it right, and stands behind the work.

Our Story

Why We Started Renovo

Renovo Surface Solutions was founded to fix a problem property managers across Utah kept running into: cleaning vendors who were inconsistent, hard to reach, and impossible to hold accountable. We built Renovo to be the opposite — licensed, insured, and equipped with industrial-grade equipment, with crews who treat every property like it's the one they're being judged on.

Today we serve gas stations, retail plazas, office buildings, restaurants, parking structures, and property management portfolios across 20+ cities along the Wasatch Front.

The Name

Re·no·vo /Latin/

From the Latin renovare — "to renew, restore, make new again." It's not just our name, it's the outcome we're hired to deliver on every surface we touch.

Leadership

Pivot Ngoy, Founder & Owner

Pivot Ngoy founded Renovo Surface Solutions LLC to bring a higher, more accountable standard to commercial surface care in Utah. Every crew, every piece of equipment, and every process at Renovo is built around one goal: a property that looks unmistakably well cared for.

Under Pivot's leadership, Renovo has grown into a full-service surface restoration and facility care company serving property managers across the Wasatch Front — without losing the accountability and responsiveness of an owner-led business.
